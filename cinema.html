Cinema

Cinema is a web based OTT platform for streaming movies and TV shows. Cinema is Designed keeping in mind that TV shows and Movies of multiple Languages will be streamed on this Platform.

Cinema was completely designed on Figma and the motivation and thought behind designing Cinema is the rising usage of OTT Platforms. The UI/UX is designed as such that the usabilty is as simple as possible without loosing the touch of modern elements.

Problem Solving

Design Thought

A great emphasis was placed on the personalization of the content and adaptability for the viewer's needs. Extensive customer research that included quantitative surveys and qualitative interviews. These findings acted as a guiding principle during the whole design process.

I also wanted to provide a smooth experience. The content on the site is designed so that every viewer group can easily find the relevant information to them.
